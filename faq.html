Why I am not the only owner of my data while using services of popular providers? +
Every digital service provider in the modern world has a copy of your data, whether it's social networks, e-mail or software providers, banks or credit card provider, etc. This makes it a shadow owner of your data and metadata. Some agreements on "storage and use" are easily violated, the notion of bank secrecy is forgotten, thus your data is being resold, processed by algorithms and sometimes used for various purposes. Also in a modern network, your digital assets are not protected from being copied and resold by others without your knowledge or permission.
What does true ownership in digital space mean?
+
It means that only you are the sole owner of your data, responsible for it and use at your discretion. We offer you all necessary tools in order to protect your content inside the network and its distribution on your terms. Also, we, as a software or a cloud storage provider, do not have access to your data, so we can not use what we do not have.
How can I be sure that in Xmesh my data is safe and cannot be stolen by anyone, including you?
+
All your data appears and is stored on the server encrypted. Only you own the key to see and unlock it.
How can you guarantee that the data won’t be lost or stolen while transferring to others? +
When you send a file, message or something else, the content is encrypted and cipher bytes are send to a recipient. Only the recipient and the sender have the encryption key that they can use to look or unlock the digital assets. We do not see who is sending and what is sent.
How is intellectual property protected in Xmesh? +
We provide you with necessary tools for storing and protecting your data and intellectual property inside Xmesh. As soon as you upload documents or files to your Storage and set up access to the files you store you protect yourself from any unwanted copying, undesirable use of your content (links to services, goods, inventory or media content) without your knowledge of other accounts within the network.
Is Xmesh free? +
In order to chat, pay, and store your data inside the network we offer you 5 applications (5 in 1) that are absolutely free for using. There are no transaction fees when using the wallet for making payments or funds transfers within the network. As our core, we use libre software GPL v3 that everyone can use for their own purposes.
There is no such thing as a free lunch! How do you earn money? +
We take small subscription that you pay for storage you use for keeping your files, links, offers, etc. You pay only for amount you use. Simple and fair.
Why should I prove my identity while registering in Xmesh? +
After you installed the app, you get the wallet linked to your account, that you can use immediately for receiving money and using it inside the network, that is why you should prove your identity before receiving or send something. It’s like when you open a bank account.
Why is authentication so important fоr providers inside? +
Xmesh itself is built on the principles of P2P users’ identification. Providers of many services comply with the law "know your customer", therefore they are required to ask passport or other documents and licenses from users. We offer a technology for user identification. It can be a real time identification or access to your documents stored on your site.
Why is X Wallet safer then other e-wallets? +
Wallet is end-to-end encrypted, making it very resistant to hackers, malicious software and fraud. It is impossible to withdraw funds or pay for something without your consent or knowledge. The wallet and its data are controlled only by you, it can not be blocked. All funds reflected on your Current account are under the bank guarantee and can be withdrawn to your bank account. All transfers (transactions) are protected by the built-in constructor that can guarantee fulfillment of obligations for both parties and protection against potential fraud.
What functions does wallet offers inside Xmesh? +
Current account has the functions of refill, storage (limited amount), making payments and withdrawal of funds to a bank account. All funds reflected on your wallets can be instantly used inside the network without any commissions for both the receiving and sending parties. You are a sole owner of your funds and you can issue loans on your terms and protect yourself with built-in smart contracts from potential fraud. Credit account is used for receiving funds sent by customers as a prepayment for the future service. These funds can be used by the provider as a guaranteed payment to their counterparties who take part in this service, which significantly saves money and time of the provider. Such transactions are protected from potential fraud by the built-in escrow service, which guarantees the fulfillment of obligations for all parties.
